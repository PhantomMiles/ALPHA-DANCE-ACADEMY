About Alphas

Meet the Alphas—our dedicated team of coaches at Alpha Dance Academy. Each instructor brings unique expertise, passion, and commitment to helping every student grow as a dancer and as an individual. Our coaches are carefully selected for their professionalism, creativity, and ability to inspire, ensuring a supportive and motivating environment for all. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced dancer, our Alphas are here to guide you on your dance journey and help you achieve your goals.

Our Current Alphas

These are the current brand instructors that will be taking our classes. They are 7 in number with two on probation. They were chosen due to their commitment to the brand as well as their dedication to the brand's growth in all aspects. Annual restructuring will determine the next set of Alphas that will be introduced.

Chekwas Michael Okorie

Chekwas Michael Okorie is the founder and CEO of Alpha Dance Academy, holding the distinguished position of 001 in rank. His vision, leadership, and passion for dance have shaped the academy’s culture and growth. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, he inspires both coaches and students to reach their highest potential. As a mentor and role model, Chekwas continually drives the team forward, fostering creativity, discipline, and unity among the Alphas. His dedication ensures that Alpha Dance Academy remains a beacon of talent and opportunity in the dance community.

Chinweze Chimamanda Mmesoma

Chinweze Chimamanda Mmesoma (Manda) is known for her resilience and intelligence, both on and off the dance floor. As an Alpha, she brings a unique blend of determination and creativity to every class. Her primary style is Afro dance, where her energy and expressive movements inspire students to embrace their own individuality. Chimamanda’s smart approach to choreography and teaching helps dancers of all levels grow in confidence and skill. Her commitment to excellence and her positive spirit make her an invaluable member of the Alpha Dance Academy team.

Obinna Joseph Sylvester

Obinna Joseph Sylvester, also known as HyperActive, is a dynamic hip-hop dancer and the current second-in-command at Alpha Dance Academy, holding the prestigious rank of 002. Renowned for his high energy, precision, and innovative choreography, HyperActive brings a vibrant presence to every class and performance. His leadership skills and dedication to the academy’s mission make him an essential pillar of the Alpha team. As a mentor, he motivates students to push their boundaries and embrace the expressive power of hip-hop. Obinna’s commitment to excellence and his supportive approach help foster a collaborative and inspiring environment for both coaches and dancers.

Ezeja Samson Chigozie

Ezeja Samson Chigozie, also known as Sam1, is celebrated for his versatility and dynamic presence at Alpha Dance Academy. While his primary focus is on cultural and Makossa dance styles, Sam1’s adaptability matches that of HyperActive, allowing him to excel across a wide range of genres. His creative approach fuses traditional rhythms with modern techniques, making each class engaging and inspiring. Sam1’s dedication to cultural expression and his ability to connect with dancers of all levels make him an invaluable member of the team, encouraging students to embrace both heritage and innovation in their dance journey.

Ignatius Wisdom

Ignatius Wisdom, popularly known as Wizzy, is recognized for his infectious energy and mastery of street afro and mara dance styles. His vibrant personality and creative choreography bring a fresh, urban edge to every class. Wizzy’s ability to connect with students and break down complex moves makes him a favorite among dancers of all levels. His passion for dance and commitment to growth inspire those around him, while his innovative approach ensures that every session is dynamic and engaging. As an Alpha, Wizzy embodies the spirit of versatility, motivation, and community at Alpha Dance Academy.

Michael Ndudi

Michael Ndudi, also known as Evil Sky or E-Sky, is a dynamic force at Alpha Dance Academy. Specializing in Hip hop and Afro pop, E-Sky is renowned for his sharp technique and creative flair. He forms a powerful duo with HyperActive, especially when it comes to pop and lock, where their synergy and precision captivate audiences and inspire students. E-Sky’s passion for dance, combined with his collaborative spirit, makes him a driving influence in the academy. His dedication to pushing boundaries and supporting his peers ensures that every class is both challenging and motivating for dancers of all levels.

Emekaogbu Miracle

Emekaogbu Miracle, known as Miraz, is celebrated for his versatility and adaptability on the dance floor. While he is skilled in various dance genres, his main style is Afro, where his creativity and energy truly shine. Miraz’s dynamic teaching approach and passion for dance inspire students to explore their own potential and embrace new challenges. His commitment to growth and his supportive attitude make him a valued member of the Alpha Dance Academy team, motivating dancers of all levels to excel and enjoy their dance journey.

Ladipoe Stella-maris

Ladipoe Stella-maris is often regarded as the unofficial twin of Manda, sharing a similar drive and passion for dance. Known for her versatility, Stella-maris can adapt to a variety of dance styles and always brings energy and intensity to every performance. Her ability to "bring the heat" when needed makes her a dynamic presence in the studio. Stella-maris inspires her peers and students alike with her commitment, creativity, and willingness to take on new challenges, making her an invaluable member of the Alpha Dance Academy team.

Afro Zito

Afro Zito is a vibrant and charismatic dance coach whose passion for Afrobeat and contemporary dance styles shines through in every class. Known for his infectious enthusiasm and innovative choreography, Zito creates an energetic and inclusive environment where students feel empowered to express themselves. His deep understanding of rhythm and movement, combined with a talent for teaching, helps dancers of all levels unlock their potential. Afro Zito’s commitment to fostering creativity and confidence makes him a beloved mentor and a driving force behind the dynamic spirit of Alpha Dance Academy.
